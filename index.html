Welcome to the Official Rock, Paper, Scissors Tournament

This was just a project for fun that I created in order to test my web development and physics skills. It plays just like rock, paper, scissors but this time without the need for human interaction. What better way is there to spend your time if not watching a bunch of rock, paper and scissor pixels battle it out on a screen?

All this combined with some wacky perfectly ellastic collisions makes for some unpredictable interactions and makes it so that you can never tell who will win until the very last moment.
